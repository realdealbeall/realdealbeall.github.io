Grant Wood Art Gallery

Anamosa, IA: Birth Place of Grant Wood

The Grant Wood Art Gallery displays prints of Grant Wood's works along with art from local artists. Our mission is to celebrate the legacy of Grant Wood by encouraging an appreciation of art in the local community and nurturing new artists.
